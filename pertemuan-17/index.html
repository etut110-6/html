history of japan

The history of Japan is rooted in ancient civilizations influenced by China and Korea, later evolving into an empire with an imperial system that still exists today. The samurai era introduced the Bushido tradition that valued honor, while the Edo period marked a long era of isolation. After the Meiji Restoration in the 19th century, Japan transformed into a modern nation and an industrial power, eventually becoming one of the world's centers of culture, technology, and economy
